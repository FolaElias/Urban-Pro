Property Development

Our projects range from luxury apartments and gated communities to office complexes and retail hubs. Each development is designed to meet the highest standards of quality and innovation.

At UrbanPro Housing, every property development project begins with a deep understanding of the market, the environment, and the end user. We integrate sustainable building practices into every stage — from land acquisition and site planning through to construction and handover.

Residential Development

Commercial Development

Mixed-Use Projects

Sustainable Building

Civil Eng. Contracting

Our team of skilled civil engineers and contractors provides end-to-end construction and infrastructure development services. From site preparation and road construction to drainage systems and utility installations, we ensure that every project is executed with precision and efficiency.

UrbanPro Housing's civil engineering division brings deep technical expertise to every engagement. We manage complex construction challenges — from geotechnical assessments and earthworks to structural builds and infrastructure commissioning — delivering projects that stand the test of time.

Site Preparation

Comprehensive land clearing, levelling, and geotechnical assessments to establish a safe and stable foundation for every project.

Road Construction

Design and construction of access roads, internal estate roads, and public highway projects built to engineering specifications.

Drainage Systems

Installation of stormwater drainage, sewage networks, and flood management infrastructure to protect properties and communities.

Utility Installations

Coordination and installation of water supply, electrical networks, telecommunications, and gas infrastructure for new developments.

Our Engineering Expertise

Our civil engineering team leverages modern surveying equipment, BIM software, and construction management systems to deliver infrastructure projects that are precise, compliant, and cost-effective. We coordinate seamlessly with local authorities, utility companies, and project stakeholders to minimise delays.

Project Execution Standards

Every civil engineering contract is governed by a strict quality management plan. Our site supervisors conduct daily inspections, and third-party testing labs verify material specifications at every critical stage of construction.

Renovation & Remodeling

We transform existing structures into modern, high-performance spaces tailored to your lifestyle or business needs. Our renovation and remodeling services breathe new life into aging properties — improving functionality, enhancing aesthetics, and significantly increasing market value.

Whether you are upgrading a private residence, refurbishing a commercial office, or undertaking a full structural overhaul, UrbanPro Housing's renovation team delivers results that exceed expectations while minimising disruption to your daily operations.

Interior Renovation

Complete interior makeovers covering flooring, ceilings, partitioning, finishes, and fixtures to modernise your living or working space.

Structural Remodeling

Safe and engineered structural alterations including load-bearing wall modifications, room additions, and floor plan reconfigurations.

Commercial Fit-Out

Turnkey office, retail, and hospitality fit-outs designed to align with your brand identity and operational workflow.

Property Restoration

Careful restoration of heritage and aged properties, preserving their character while upgrading essential systems and finishes.

Our Renovation Process

Every renovation begins with a detailed site assessment and client consultation. Our team prepares a scope of works, material schedule, and project timeline before a single tool is lifted. We coordinate all trades — carpentry, plumbing, electrical, plastering, painting — under a single contract for a seamless and stress-free experience.

Why Renovate with UrbanPro?

Renovation is one of the most cost-effective ways to increase the value and liveability of a property. UrbanPro Housing brings construction expertise, project management discipline, and design sensitivity to every renovation — ensuring the finished result is both beautiful and built to last.

Joint Venture Partnership

UrbanPro Housing Investment Limited collaborates with landowners, investors, and developers to create mutually beneficial partnerships. Through joint ventures, we combine resources and expertise to deliver high-impact real estate projects that generate value for all stakeholders.

We believe the most successful real estate projects are built on trust, shared vision, and complementary strengths. Our joint venture model is structured to be transparent, equitable, and aligned with the goals of every partner — from the initial agreement through to project completion and profit distribution.

Land Acquisition Partnerships

We partner with landowners who contribute land while UrbanPro provides the capital, design, construction, and marketing expertise to develop and sell the completed project.

Investment Collaboration

Financial investors co-invest alongside UrbanPro in specific projects, sharing returns proportional to their stake while benefiting from our full development management capability.

Developer Partnerships

Collaborations with other developers and contractors to pool technical resources, labour capacity, and market access for large-scale or complex projects.

Profit Sharing Models

Clear and agreed-upon profit-sharing structures negotiated upfront, ensuring all parties understand their returns before any commitment is made.

Architectural Design

Our architectural design services blend creativity with engineering precision to deliver spaces that are visually striking, functionally superior, and built for longevity. From initial concept sketches to detailed construction drawings, our architects guide every project with meticulous attention to detail and a deep commitment to client vision.

UrbanPro Housing's design philosophy is rooted in the belief that great architecture serves people. Every structure we design is responsive to its context — climate, culture, community — while meeting the highest standards of spatial efficiency, structural integrity, and aesthetic excellence.

Concept Design

Translating client briefs and site conditions into compelling design concepts that establish the vision, form, and character of the project from day one.

Structural Planning

Detailed architectural and structural drawings that define every element of the building — from foundations to roof — ready for regulatory approval and construction.

Interior Architecture

Thoughtful interior spatial design covering floor plans, material palettes, lighting strategies, and built-in fixtures that elevate the user experience.

Landscape Design

Integrated landscape and external environment design — driveways, planting, lighting, and outdoor amenities — that complement and enhance the built form.

Our Design Philosophy

We approach every design challenge with curiosity and rigour. Our architects draw on local context, environmental conditions, and the latest advances in building technology to produce designs that are not only beautiful but also practical and sustainable. We use 3D modelling and BIM tools to bring designs to life before construction begins, helping clients visualise and refine their spaces with confidence.

From Vision to Reality

Good design is only valuable when it can be built. UrbanPro's architectural team works hand-in-hand with our civil engineering and construction divisions to ensure every design is constructable, compliant, and delivered as intended — bridging the gap between drawing board and completed building.

  • Innovative and context-responsive concepts
  • Full regulatory submission drawings
  • 3D visualisation and BIM modelling
  • Interior architecture & landscape design
  • Integrated with construction team
  • Compliance with all building codes
  • Sustainable design principles
