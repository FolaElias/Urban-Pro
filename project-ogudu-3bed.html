Contemporary Elegance & Minimalist Beauty

Nestled in the prestigious and highly secure Ogudu GRA, Lagos, this project features contemporary 3-Bedroom Semi-Detached Duplexes. Designed with a minimalist design philosophy, the homes emphasize clean architectural lines, open floor plans, and functional spaces that maximize natural light. It offers a peaceful retreat for families seeking premium urban living combined with high privacy, premium finishes, and first-rate infrastructure.

  • Minimalist Interiors: Sleek, clutter-free layouts with handleless cabinets, recessed lighting, and a cohesive neutral color palette.
  • Premium Flooring: Finished with high-end polished porcelain tiles in living spaces and engineered wood in bedrooms.
  • Eco-Friendly Systems: Outfitted with energy-efficient LED lighting, solar panel conduits, and low-flow water fixtures.
  • Luxury Bathrooms: Features wall-hung toilets, walk-in rain showers, and premium vanity mirrors with integrated lighting.
  • Security & Convenience: Video doorbells, secure private parking, automated gate entrance, and clean borehole water supply.
